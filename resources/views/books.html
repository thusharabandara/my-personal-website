Books I have read

“Reading makes a full man” is a famous quote by the English philosopher Sir Francis Bacon and no other inspirational words were said, in order to defeat it. Based on Thushara’s perspective, pretty much everything in the world can be learned and understood by reading books. Of course, some books are interesting and some are boring. It doesn’t matter what the genre you would love to read, there is nothing more relaxing than spending with an interesting book in a calm place alone. It brings him to a world he couldn’t imagine. However, all the other subject related books really help him to understand deep concepts behind the things hard to understand. As a lecturer, he has been reading books as a part of his daily schedule to teach students theories of Computer Science and other motivational factors.

All the books are listed according to the year he read them.
